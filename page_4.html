ABOUT MATHEW DELL

Hi guys! Dell here.

Born on the African plains beneath the stars, midwifed by a baboon witchdoctor, I stepped boldly into the world of art at the ripe old age of 8. After stowing away in a colonial landing vessel and living off of unripened bananas for 6 months in its cargo hold, I took my first steps onto the land of the long white cloud, Aotearoa, at age 15.

I have been absorbed in progressing my artistic abilities ever since, with 10 years dedicated to drawing photorealism, which lead me to travel and work as a freelance artist in the US after finishing my postgraduate studies as a Sound Engineer and Studio Producer in NZ.

In my time overseas, studying under some of the Godfathers of visionary art, my photorealist background developed organically into something far more psychedelic and surreal, and this style has fueled my passion for creative expression - which is why and how I find myself tattooing, searching for more outlets to create beauty with art.

Tattoo has pushed me technically and creatively in ways I could only imagine, and I truly love working with as many styles and ideas as possible to keep my hand and mind dynamic and ready for anything, while I work towards my own unique take on 'Forever Art' on the body.

Can't help but admit though, the 'big, bold and black' origins of tattoo deeply appeal to me, seconded only by my love for vibrant colour.

When I'm not tattooing, drawing or painting, you will find me on the mats at Jiu Jitsu, or fire dancing when the sun sets!
